Frequently Asked Questions

What is Battle Online?

Battle Online is an online platform where you can play various games against friends or AI. Join tournaments, challenge others, and enjoy epic battles!

How do I start playing a game?

You can start playing by visiting the game lobby and selecting the game you want to play. You can also join tournaments or play offline.

How can I contact support?

For support, you can reach out to us via the contact page or email us at support@battleonline.com.
